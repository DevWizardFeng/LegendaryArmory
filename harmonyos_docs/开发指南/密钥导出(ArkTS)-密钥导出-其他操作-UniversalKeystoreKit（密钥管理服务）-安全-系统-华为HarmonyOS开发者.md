<h1 _ngcontent-qqr-c119="" class="doc-title ng-star-inserted" title="密钥导出(ArkTS)"> 密钥导出(ArkTS) </h1>

<div _ngcontent-qqr-c106="" auitextselectionexpansion="" class="markdown-body ng-star-inserted" style="position: relative;">   <div>    <p>业务需要获取持久化存储的非对称密钥的公钥时使用，当前支持ECC/RSA/ED25519/X25519/SM2的公钥导出。</p>    <div><div class="hw-editor-tip info"><div class="title">说明</div><div class="content">      <p>轻量级智能穿戴仅支持RSA公钥导出。</p>     </div></div></div>    <div class="tiledSection">     <h2 id="开发步骤">开发步骤<i class="anchor-icon anchor-icon-link" anchorid="开发步骤" tips="复制节点链接"></i></h2>          <ol>      <li>       <p>指定密钥别名，密钥别名命名规范参考<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-guides/huks-key-generation-overview">密钥生成介绍及算法规格</a>。</p></li>      <li>       <p>调用接口<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/js-apis-huks#huksexportkeyitem9" target="_blank">exportKeyItem</a>，传入参数keyAlias和options。options为预留参数，当前可传入空。</p></li>      <li>       <p>返回值为<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/js-apis-huks#huksreturnresult9" target="_blank">HuksReturnResult</a>类型对象，获取的公钥明文在outData字段中，以标准的X.509规范的DER格式封装，具体请参考<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-guides/huks-concepts#公钥材料格式">公钥材料格式</a>。</p></li>
