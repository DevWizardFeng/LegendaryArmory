<div _ngcontent-erp-c106="" auitextselectionexpansion="" class="markdown-body ng-star-inserted" style="position: relative;"> <div><p>在开始应用开发前，需要先完成以下准备工作。</p> <div class="tiledSection"><h2 href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/start/rna-0000001062530373" target="_blank">实名认证</a>，从而享受联盟开放的各类能力和服务。</p> </div> <div class="tiledSection"><h2 id="section13566816173114">创建应用<i target="_blank">AppGallery Connect</a>（简称AGC）上，参考<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/app/agc-help-create-project-0000002242804048" target="_blank">创建项目</a>和<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/app/agc-help-create-app-0000002247955506" target="_blank">创建应用</a>完成<strong>HarmonyOS</strong>应用的创建，从而使用各类服务。</p> </div> <div href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/download/" target="_blank">DevEco Studio</a>。具体安装指导请参见<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-guides/ide-software-install" target="_blank">安装DevEco Studio</a>。</p> </div> <div class="tiledSection"><h2 id="section952017213481">使用DevEco Studio创建应用工程<i class="anchor-icon anchor-icon-link" anchorid="section952017213481"
