<h1 _ngcontent-ale-c119="" class="doc-title ng-star-inserted" title="应用评论服务"> 应用评论服务 </h1>

<div _ngcontent-ale-c106="" auitextselectionexpansion="" class="markdown-body ng-star-inserted" style="position: relative;">   <div>    <p>通过应用评论服务，用户无需进入应用市场应用详情页，可以直接在应用内进行评论。</p>    <div><div class="hw-editor-tip info"><div class="title">说明</div><div class="content">      <p>从版本6.0.0(20)开始，支持拉起应用评论弹框。</p>     </div></div></div>    <div class="tiledSection">     <h2 id="section10343164404617">场景介绍<i class="anchor-icon anchor-icon-link" anchorid="section10343164404617" tips="复制节点链接"></i></h2>          <ul>      <li>拉起应用评论弹框       <p>开发者可以通过该接口拉起应用评论弹窗对应用进行评分及评论，无需进入应用市场应用详情页进行评论。</p></li>     </ul>     <p><span><img height="481.426351" originheight="784" originwidth="372" title="点击放大" width="228.4275"></span></p>     <p></p>    </div>    <div class="tiledSection">     <h2 id="section95351243153212">业务流程<i class="anchor-icon anchor-icon-link" anchorid="section95351243153212" tips="复制节点链接"></i></h2>          <p><span><img height="145.63500000000002" originheight="330" originwidth="1181" src="https://alliance-communityfile-drcn.dbankcdn.com/FileServer/getFile/cmtyPub/011/111/111/0000000000011111111.20251211114525.33490573437381369174904117261922:50001231000000:2800:211C5F090535D21BDBC0F2D4E0709275D0630B0F8848FC08E5828FD74F55895D.png" title="点击放大" width="523.6875"></span></p>    </div>    <ol>     <li>用户需要在应用内评论应用。</li>     <li>应用调用showCommentDialog接口拉起应用评论弹窗。</li>     <li>AppGalleryKit返回接口调用结果给应用。</li>     <li>应用返回评论窗口给用户。</li>    </ol>    <div class="tiledSection">     <h2 id="section121516161618">约束与限制<i class="anchor-icon anchor-icon-link" anchorid="section121516161618" tips="复制节点链接"></i></h2>
