<li>       <p>指定待签名的明文数据。</p></li>      <li>       <p>调用<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/capi-native-huks-param-h#oh_huks_initparamset" href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/capi-native-huks-api-h#oh_huks_finishsession" target="_blank">OH_Huks_FinishSession</a>结束密钥会话，获取签名signature。</p></li>     </ol>     <p><strong>验签</strong></p>     <ol>      <li>       <p>获取密钥别名。</p></li>      <li>       <p>获取待验证的签名signature。</p></li>      <li>       <p>调用<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/capi-native-huks-param-h#oh_huks_initparamset" target="_blank">OH_Huks_InitParamSet</a>指定<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/capi-native-huks-param-h#oh_huks_initparamset" target="_blank">算法参数配置</a>。</p></li>      <li>       <p>调用<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/capi-native-huks-api-h#oh_huks_initsession" target="_blank">OH_Huks_InitSession</a>初始化密钥会话，并获取会话的句柄handle。</p></li>      <li>       <p>调用<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/capi-native-huks-api-h#oh_huks_updatesession" target="_blank">OH_Huks_UpdateSession</a>更新密钥会话。</p></li>      <li>       <p>调用<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/capi-native-huks-api-h#oh_huks_finishsession" target="_blank">OH_Huks_FinishSession</a>结束密钥会话，验证签名。</p></li>     </ol>
