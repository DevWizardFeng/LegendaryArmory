<h1 _ngcontent-xfu-c119="" class="doc-title ng-star-inserted" title="获取密钥属性(ArkTS)"> 获取密钥属性(ArkTS) </h1>

<div _ngcontent-xfu-c106="" auitextselectionexpansion="" class="markdown-body ng-star-inserted" style="position: relative;">   <div>    <p>HUKS提供了接口供业务获取指定密钥的相关属性。在获取指定密钥属性前，需要确保已在HUKS中生成或导入持久化存储的密钥。</p>    <div><div class="hw-editor-tip info"><div class="title">说明</div><div class="content">      <p>轻量级智能穿戴不支持获取密钥属性功能。</p>     </div></div></div>    <div class="tiledSection">     <h2 id="开发步骤">开发步骤<i class="anchor-icon anchor-icon-link" anchorid="开发步骤" tips="复制节点链接"></i></h2>          <ol>      <li>       <p>指定待查询的密钥别名keyAlias，密钥别名最大长度为128字节。</p></li>      <li>       <p>调用接口<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/js-apis-huks#huksgetkeyitemproperties9" target="_blank">getKeyItemProperties</a>，传入参数keyAlias和options。options为预留参数，当前可传入空。</p></li>      <li>       <p>返回值为<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/js-apis-huks#huksreturnresult9" target="_blank">HuksReturnResult</a>类型对象，获取的属性集在properties字段中。</p></li>     </ol>     <div
