<h1 _ngcontent-voe-c119="" class="doc-title ng-star-inserted" title="认证过程中取消认证"> 认证过程中取消认证 </h1>

<div _ngcontent-voe-c106="" auitextselectionexpansion="" class="markdown-body ng-star-inserted" style="position: relative;">   <div>    <p>统一用户认证框架提供了cancel接口，当应用在认证过程中，需要取消认证时可调用该接口。</p>    <div class="tiledSection">     <h2 id="接口说明">接口说明<i class="anchor-icon anchor-icon-link" anchorid="接口说明" tips="复制节点链接"></i></h2>          <p>具体参数、返回值、错误码等描述，请参考对应的<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/js-apis-useriam-userauth#cancel10" target="_blank">API文档</a>。</p>     <p>此处仅展示了取消认证操作的接口，在取消认证前，需要先发起认证，发起认证的接口列表、详细说明可参考<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-guides/start-authentication">发起认证</a>章节和API文档。</p>     <div class="tablenoborder">      <div class="tbBox"><table class="layoutFixed idpTab">       <thead>        <tr>         <th align="left" class="cellrowborder" id="mcps1.3.2.4.1.3.1.1" valign="top" width="50%">接口名称</th>         <th align="left" class="cellrowborder" <td class="cellrowborder" valign="top" width="50%">取消本次认证操作。</td>        </tr>             </tbody></table></div>     </div>    </div>    <div class="tiledSection">     <h2 id="开发步骤">开发步骤<i class="anchor-icon anchor-icon-link" anchorid="开发步骤" tips="复制节点链接"></i></h2>          <ol>      <li>       <p><a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-guides/prerequisites#申请权限">申请权限</a>：ohos.permission.ACCESS_BIOMETRIC。</p></li>      <li>       <p>指定用户认证相关参数<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/js-apis-useriam-userauth#authparam10" target="_blank">AuthParam</a>（包括挑战值、认证类型<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/js-apis-useriam-userauth#userauthtype8" target="_blank">UserAuthType</a>列表和认证等级<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/js-apis-useriam-userauth#authtrustlevel8" target="_blank">AuthTrustLevel</a>），获取认证对象<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/js-apis-useriam-userauth#userauthinstance10" target="_blank">UserAuthInstance</a>，并调用<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/js-apis-useriam-userauth#start10" target="_blank">UserAuthInstance.start</a>发起认证。此步骤详细说明可参考<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-guides/start-authentication">发起认证</a>。</p></li>      <li>       <p>使用已经成功发起认证的UserAuthInstance对象调用<a href="https://developer.huawei.com/consumer/cn/doc/harmonyos-references/js-apis-useriam-userauth#cancel10" target="_blank">UserAuthInstance.cancel</a>接口取消本次认证。</p></li>     </ol>
