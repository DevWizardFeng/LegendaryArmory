<h1 _ngcontent-sxm-c119="" class="doc-title ng-star-inserted" title="随机生成非对称密钥对(ArkTS)"> 随机生成非对称密钥对(ArkTS) </h1>

<div _ngcontent-sxm-c106="" auitextselectionexpansion="" class="markdown-body ng-star-inserted" style="position: relative;">   <div>    <p>以RSA和SM2为例，随机生成非对称密钥对（KeyPair），并获得二进制数据。</p>
